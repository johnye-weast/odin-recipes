Spaghetti Soup

But What Is It?

Spaghetti soup is a staple of the Italian Mafia and was created to put into giant pots so
that they could smuggle tommy guns into movie theatres and dinners with important people.
In doing so they became the world's greatest (and fattest) criminals!

Another good use for this today is to hand out to kids who seem like they might want to join
the mafia as well. It is well known that this is why many kids in New York opt for their
big ol bowls. They have all kinds of tommy guns to tote around and it would be a shame
to get caught.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti Noodles
  • Marianas Trench Sauce
  • 1 Tomato
  • 1 Handful of Goat Meat
  • 2 Tommy Guns

Steps

  1. Throw the noodles all over the ground, unless you like noodles, in which case you should
    probably just put them in some boiling water with all of the other enemies of the mafia.
  2. Put your tomato into the marianas trench sauce. It's imperative that this sauce be fresh
    from the Marianas other wise it will probably just taste like grosser spaghetti.
  3. Once the noodles are done boiling, throw in the marianas trench sauce and say something
    along the lines of "Look here, seeee!"
  4. Sprinkle your two tommy guns on top and take it to the shebang!
