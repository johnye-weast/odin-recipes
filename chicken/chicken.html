Tuscan Blackened Chicken

But What Is It?

This chicken is pretty dank, no cap. In fact, you won't even want the cap once you've taken it
off because you will finish it in one sitting. I had my doubts that this would be the
banger that I had heard so much about but after slaving in the kitchen all night after the
gym, I knew that I was going to be jacked.

First of all, I really enjoy taking the powers of protes away from chicken. It really gets me
absolutely stoked knowing that I'm going to be like nine times the size of that chicken
after I get done with that meal. A little eatin' a little sleepin' and I am indestructable.

Ingredients

  • Gotta have the chicken
  • 2 flakes of pepper
  • Da juice
  • A shirt
  1. Lay the chicken out. When I say that I mean...punch the chicken in the face. Those are your
    gains now.
  2. Defeather the chicken and throw the body into the four flakes of pepper. You really don't
    want too many calories in this meal so don't use more than 5.
  3. Finally, pressure cook the chicken between your pecs for three seconds. Make sure to leave
    off the sleeves for this. We need all the heat we can get.
